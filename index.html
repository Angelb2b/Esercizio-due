Ingredienti

    For the Focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cUps (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive o1, plus more for the pan

    For the topping

  • 3 medium vellow onions. thinv sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Metod

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actualy knead the dougn, Thought I sometimes knead it a lew times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
